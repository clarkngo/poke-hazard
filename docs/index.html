POKÉHAZARD

 

A survival horror monster-collecting RPG. Hunt the infected, extract their genetic code, and build living weapons from their biology. The virus spreads. So do you.

// SPECIMEN CLASSIFICATIONS //

Core Features

Every system is built around one loop: hunt the infected, harvest their biology, and weaponize what you find.

PHASE 2 🧬

Viral Dex

Track every infected creature from first sighting to full capture. Three tiers — Spotted, Encountered, Captured — each unlocking more data and crafting options.

PHASE 5 ⚗️

T-Evolution

Monsters mutate through a three-stage T-Evolution chain triggered by level, viral exposure, or held items. Each stage unlocks new genetic codes and craftable abilities.

PHASE 5 🔬

Mutation Monsters

Combine two captured monsters in the Mutation Chamber. Offspring inherit the dominant viral strain and mixed stats. Conflicting strains may produce rare Chimeras.

PHASE 4 ⚡

Viral Core Extraction

Defeat named monsters to extract their Unique Genetic Code. Slot one core per weapon for a Mega Man-style active ability — Licker's Grapple, Hunter's Claw, or Tyrant's Rage.

PHASE 4 🔩

Bio-Containerization

Weapons have 1–4 Bio-Pod slots unlocked by XP. Slot compressed monsters for elemental rounds and scopes, or stack three of the same Viral Strain to trigger a weapon evolution.

PHASE 4 🫀

Living Weapons

Your weapons are biological entities. Feed them Bio-Pods to stay powerful. Starve them and they go Dormant. Watch your gun grow eyes, pulse with veins, and sprout twitching limbs.

PHASE 6 🛡️

Bio-Armor

Harvest monster biology for reactive armor. Carapace Plating blocks and breaks. Regenerative Weave slowly heals. Reactive Spikes counter-attack anything that grabs you.

PHASE 6 👁️

Sentient Accessories

Living hardware with passive effects. The Watcher Lens highlights weak points. Adrenaline Glands trigger speed bursts near death. Pheromone Diffusers mask you from low-tier zombies.

PHASE 6 ☣️

Viral Load

Every piece of bio-gear adds Viral Load to your body. Exceed the cap and mutation begins — reduced max HP, random debuffs. More power always comes at a cost.

How It Works

Three interlocking systems that form the core gameplay loop.

01

The Living Weapon Loop

Weapons are alive and hungry. Hunt monsters, compress them into Bio-Pods, and feed your weapon to keep its abilities active. Starvation sends it Dormant until you hunt again.

HUNT › CAPTURE › COMPRESS › FEED › POWER › DORMANT ↺
